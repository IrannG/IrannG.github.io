College

New Mexico Tech

I began my first year of college in the Fall of 2018 at the New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology in Socorro, New Mexico. Here, I am planning on graduating in the spring of 2022 with a Bachelor's of Science in Physics with a focus on astrophysics. My plan is to work on research for near Earth objects and on an efficient collision prevention system. Space and the technology required to explore it is what I am really pationate about. I would also love to teach physics to high school students and below to motivate them into thinking big and tackling difficult problems head-on. I have always enjoyed challenging myself inside and outside of school, and I would like to think I can deliver that same passion to kids. Below, I show some groups and programs I joined that have greatly impacted my way of thinking about life in general and academia in particular.

Santa Fe

Breakthrough Santa Fe is a 7th-12th grade summer program that provides support for students who are the first in their families to attend college.
